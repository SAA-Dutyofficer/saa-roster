📖 System Guide

Getting Started

1. Go to Settings (Dashboard) — set the month and year you want to generate
2. Go to Staff tab — add all your staff with their pattern, group and cycle start position
3. Go to Leaves tab — add any leave entries for the month (or year for military)
4. Click ⚡ Generate Roster — the system builds the full month automatically
5. Check Coverage tab — fix any gaps by clicking cells on the Roster
6. Use Export tab — download CSV/Excel/PDF for admin or IT

Shift Patterns

  • 3W3X — 3 days on, 3 days off. Default cycle: M,M,M → off → A,A,A → off → N,N,N → off (18-day cycle)
  • 6W4X — 6 days on, 4 days off (old pattern). Cycle: M,M,A,A,N,N → 4 off days
  • 4W3X — 4 days on, 3 days off
  • General Duty — Mon to Thu = G, Fri/Sat/Sun = off
  • WFH/Remote — same as General Duty but shown as WFH
  • Manual — you set each day individually by clicking cells

Cycle Start Position

This tells the system where in the cycle each staff member is on Day 1 of the month.

  • 1M = Starting their 1st Morning shift
  • 2M = Starting their 2nd Morning shift (mid-block)
  • 1X = Starting on an off day
  • 1A = Starting their 1st Afternoon shift
  • 1N = Starting their 1st Night shift

Each month, update the cycle position based on where they left off at end of previous month.

Shift Lock Options

  • DEFAULT — Follows the 18-day rotation automatically (M→A→N→repeat)
  • LOCKED M/A/N — Always that shift, never changes

Leave Rules

  • All leave types (except Military) — only working/duty days counted. Off days within leave period show as x and are NOT counted
  • Military Service — ALL calendar days counted, including weekends and off days, no exceptions
  • Example: 9 days leave with 3 off days = 6 leave days counted, 3 show as x

Coverage Dashboard

  • GAP — Zero DOs on that shift. Must fix immediately
  • LOW — Below minimum threshold you set
  • OK — At minimum threshold
  • OVER — Above minimum (good — extra coverage)
  • General duty and WFH staff are NOT counted in shift coverage
  • All days shown including weekends

User Roles

  • Admin — Full access to everything
  • Supervisor — Can add/edit staff and leaves, edit roster cells, view coverage and export
  • Staff — Roster tab only, read-only. Can toggle between their own schedule and full roster

Home Standby (HSB)

  • HM — Home Standby Morning (shown in neon green)
  • HA — Home Standby Afternoon (shown in neon green)
  • HSB staff are NOT counted as shift coverage — they are backup only
  • Assign by clicking any cell on the roster and selecting HM or HA
